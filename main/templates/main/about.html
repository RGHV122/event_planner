About Us

The foundation of love and a lifelong bond, a wedding is definitely one of the most memorable days of life. Here at Heavenly Hitched, we assure you your perfect day. Nothing shorter than a dreamy, finest and unforgettable experience. At heavenly hitched, we not only manage a wedding, but we curate it thread by thread with love.

Heavenly Hitched initiated in 2020 by young, enthusiastic, creative and experienced event managers in New Delhi. "Dilwalon ki Delhi" as it is fondly called is famous for its big fat Indian weddings, From venue selection to theme decor, from authentic food to paradise like outdoors, from tailor made invites to the best musical vaganza, we curate everything. We work intimately with the families to give them the best memories to cherish.

Heavenly Hitched delivers highly customized, affordable, and a trendy wedding. We are committed to provide a hassle free wedding so that couples and families can focus on the special occasion and only reminisce the profound happiness, not the stress. We assure you to make your wedding picture perfect. We promise to value every penny you spend and to design your dream wedding for an unforgettable lifetime experience
